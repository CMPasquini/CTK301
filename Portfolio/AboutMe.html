Welcome to my about me page!

My name is Colton Pasquini, and I attend ISU with hopes of attaining a degree in interdisciplinary technologies. Interdisciplinary technologies consists of art, music, and game design. I hope to be able to use this degree to become an artist for something similar to cartoons or videogames. I have been passionate about my interest in videogames and cartoons since I was very little, and during high school I began working on my art skills, and due to that I became interested in wanting to work in the field of cartooning or animating. Some of the pieces in my portfolio are pieces from high school, leading up to college where I continue to work on art.
